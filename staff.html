Our Mission is to provide high quality education and to develop motivated, confident and responsible individuals who aspire to achieve their full potential.

We believe that a quantitative understanding of Earth based on field measurements, laboratory experiments and data analysis are highly important for coping with challenges that humanity faces nowadays.

For these reasons we strive to prepare all students to become lifelong learners and responsible citizens ready to meet the challenges of the future.

Breaking barriers by providing a culture of learning with 21st century educational tools, experiences, and mentors to empower all students, ensuring higher education readiness.
