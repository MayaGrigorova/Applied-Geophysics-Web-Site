Download our Undergraduate Program

Undergraduate students interested in Geophysics can find an exciting learning environment in the Department of Geophysics.

The Geophysics curriculum provides field-based classes, engaging research opportunities, and excellent preparation for careers in academia, industry, and government.
  • Detailed information on these opportunities is available in the Erasmus+ Programme Guide
  • Educational Travel programs
  • SEG Student Chapters

  • Set to last until 2020, Erasmus+ doesn't just have opportunities for students. Merging seven prior programmes, it has opportunities for a wide variety of individuals and organisations.

  • Experiential learning involves students taking part in a first-hand experience, and for many subjects the most effective way to engage students in a topic is to travel to a destination which features elements of the curriculum that you are studying and coming face to face with the subject.

  • With 113 active chapters in 32 countries, SEG Student Chapters seek to bring together like-minded individuals interested in better understanding our resource-rich environment and the minerals it contains.
