Geophysics -

Understanding Earth

Study for better future

"The Earth is what we all have in common."

- Wendell Berry

Geophysicists study Earth processes using laboratory experiments, computational and theoretical modeling, remote imaging, and direct observation.Using high precision imaging and data analysis, our research focus on earthquakes, volcanoes, environmental hazards, energy, resources and Earth's structure and geodynamics.
